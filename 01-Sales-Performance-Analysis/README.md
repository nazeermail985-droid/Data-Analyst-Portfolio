# Sales Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze sales data to identify trends, top-performing products, customer behavior, and regional performance using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

## Business Insights

- West Region generated the highest sales.
- Technology category contributed the highest profit.
- Discounts above 20% significantly reduced profit.
- Sales increased during Q4 due to holiday demand.
- Repeat customers generated over 60% of total revenue.
